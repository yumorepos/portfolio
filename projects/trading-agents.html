Overview

What it is: Production-ready AI trading system using The Agency framework (50K+ GitHub stars) to coordinate four specialized agents for prediction market arbitrage.

What it does: Continuously scans 200+ Polymarket markets, identifies arbitrage opportunities, sizes positions using Kelly Criterion, executes trades via CLOB API, and monitors performance in real-time.

Why it matters: Demonstrates mastery of multi-agent architecture, financial modeling, API integration, and production deployment—all skills directly applicable to backend and quant roles.

The Problem

Manual prediction market trading requires constant monitoring, complex probability math, and split-second execution. Individual traders struggle with:

  • Market inefficiencies disappearing in seconds
  • Optimal position sizing calculations
  • Emotional decision-making under pressure
  • Tracking performance across multiple positions

Goal: Build an automated system that identifies profitable opportunities, sizes positions mathematically, and executes without emotion.

Technical Architecture

Four Specialized Agents

  1. Market Scanner: Monitors 200+ markets, detects arbitrage opportunities, completes full scan in <5 seconds
  2. Risk Manager: Applies Kelly Criterion (0.5x), enforces circuit breakers, caps positions at 5% of bankroll
  3. Execution Agent: Integrates with Polymarket CLOB API, targets <0.5% slippage, handles order placement and fills
  4. Performance Monitor: Tracks real-time P&L, calculates Sharpe ratio and drawdown, generates performance reports

Key Technical Decisions

  • The Agency Framework: Chose battle-tested framework (50K+ stars) over custom implementation for reliability
  • Kelly Criterion 0.5x: Validated against profitable $1.8K open-source system, balances growth vs risk
  • 5% Position Cap: Hard limit prevents overexposure even when Kelly suggests larger bets
  • Max 3 Concurrent: Focus on high-conviction opportunities, avoid portfolio fragmentation

Challenges & Solutions

Challenge 1: Position Sizing

Problem: Standard Kelly Criterion can suggest aggressive position sizes that risk ruin.

Solution: Implemented Kelly 0.5x (half-Kelly) plus hard 5% cap, validated against profitable open-source system.

Challenge 2: Market Inefficiencies

Problem: Arbitrage opportunities disappear in seconds as other traders spot them.

Solution: Optimized market scanner to <5s full scan, immediate execution when opportunity detected.

Challenge 3: API Reliability

Problem: Prediction market APIs can fail during high-volume periods.

Solution: Comprehensive error handling, retry logic, fallback to read-only mode if writes fail.
